小書齋創立於屏東的一個小鎮-潮州。在小鎮默默推廣及耕耘書畫藝術創作，每張作品皆為親手書寫及獨家創作，
希望可以讓更多人感受書畫藝術的創作活力及手寫的溫度。

Xiaoshuzhai was founded in a small town called Chaozhou in Pingtung. Quietly promoting and cultivating calligraphy and painting art in the town, each piece is handwritten and uniquely created. We hope to let more people experience the vitality of artistic creation and the warmth of handwritten works.
